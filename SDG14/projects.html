Ocean Cleanup Project
- Aims to remove plastic from the world's oceans.
- Focuses on cleaning up the Great Pacific Garbage Patch.


Seacology
- Works to protect coral reefs and island ecosystems.
- Partners with local communities for sustainable development.


Coral Triangle Initiative
- A regional effort to conserve marine resources in the Coral Triangle.
- Involves six countries: Indonesia, Malaysia, Papua New Guinea, Philippines, Solomon Islands, and Timor-Leste.


Marine Conservation Society
- Advocates for marine wildlife protection and sustainable fishing.
- Engages in beach clean-ups and marine education programs.


Blue Carbon Initiative
- Focuses on conserving and restoring coastal and marine ecosystems that sequester carbon.
- Supports mangroves, salt marshes, and seagrasses.


Project awareness
- A global movement dedicated to ocean protection.
- Engages divers and water enthusiasts in conservation efforts.


ReefBase
- An online database and information platform for coral reef research and management.
- Supports sustainable practices and policies.


UN Decade of Ocean Science for Sustainable Development (2021-2030)
- A global initiative to enhance ocean science and promote sustainable development.
- Aims to improve ocean health and resilience.


International Whaling Commission (IWC)
- Works to protect whale populations and their habitats.
- Promotes sustainable whale watching and research.
